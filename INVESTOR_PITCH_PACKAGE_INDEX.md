## ğŸ† WINNING STRATEGIES

### What Makes a Pitch Stand Out:

**1. Confidence & Clarity**
- Know your numbers cold (30:1 LTV/CAC, $2.3T market)
- Speak with conviction (you've built the platform)
- Be concise (respect their time)

**2. Proof Over Promises**
- "We've built..." not "We will build..."
- Show the working platform
- Reference your 100+ pages of documentation

**3. Market Understanding**
- Know your competitors inside and out
- Demonstrate why you'll win
- Show market validation (research, trends)

**4. Team Credibility**
- Highlight your cybersecurity expertise
- Emphasize full-stack capabilities
- Show you can execute (platform is done)

**5. Clear Ask**
- Specific amount ($1.5M)
- Specific equity (15.8%)
- Specific use of funds (35% marketing, 30% product, 25% team)
- Specific timeline (24-month runway)
